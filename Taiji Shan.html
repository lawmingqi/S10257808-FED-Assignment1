TAIJI SHAN

Tai Chi Fan routines involve performing the graceful and flowing movements of Tai Chi while holding a fan
The fan acts as an visual extension of the body's movement
It is beautiful, fun and less intimidating compared to other weapons

"Tai Chi is all about balance" internally and physically

  • Fighting with Tai Chi involves training in techniques like strikes, kicks, joint locks, throws, and grappling
  • These techniques are executed with precision, timing, and fluidity
  • a fan can be used to block opponents' move or weapons, to deflect hits
