NINE SECTION CHAINED WHIP
九节鞭

JIU JIE BIAN

The whip chain is a tradition flexible wushu weapon style
A Chinese Chain Whip often has a flag attached to the dart in order to make spinning techniques more dramatic and easier to see.

Characteristics of the weapon

  • 9 segments connected to each other by 3-5 rings
  • The whip has a handle at one end and a metal dust at the other
  • A flag adds visual appeal and produces a rushing sound as the whip swings through the air
"Steps drunk but not the heart"

DRUNKEN SWORD
醉剑

ZUI JIAN

Appearing inebriated, stumbling and off-balance, however in reality the striking forces are clear and powerful
All parts of the body are used to block, deflect or strike
