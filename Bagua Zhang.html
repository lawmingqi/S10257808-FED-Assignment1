Bagua Zhang
八卦掌

Translated literally as "Eight Diagram Palm"

Ba Gua Zhang, along with Xing Yi Quan, and Tai Ji Quan, is one of the Chinese Internal Martial Arts
It is considered the most circular and spiraling system of wushu
Its defining characteristic is constant movement and change, and the ability to fight while being on the move

In real combat, a Bagua fighter

  • uses evasive footwork to escape the opponent's strong power
  • when the time is right the Bagua fighter counters with sudden, ferocious attack, using any part of the body
  • emphasizes constant change in response to the spontaneous and "live" quality of combat
